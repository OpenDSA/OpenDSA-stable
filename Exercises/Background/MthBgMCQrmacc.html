RAM access time is normally measured in:

"Nanoseconds (billionths of a second)"
  • "Seconds"
  • "Microseconds (millionths of a second)"
  • "Milliseconds (thousandths of a second)"

You certainly don't wait for seconds each time your computer accesses the RAM.

Compared to other things going on in the computer (such as access disk, which takes thousandths of a second), RAM access is very fast.
